No Page Has an Optimal Meta Description

Every single page on the site has a description issue - one is missing entirely, one is too short at 127 characters, and five are over 160 characters and get cut off in Google search results mid-sentence.

Why it matters Meta descriptions are the snippet that appears in Google results below your page title. When they are cut off or missing, Google writes its own - often pulling random body text that does not sell the property. Well-written descriptions that fit within 140-160 characters can increase click-through rates by 15-30%.
