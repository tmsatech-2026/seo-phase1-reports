Over-Length Meta Descriptions

Four pages have descriptions running 167-371 characters - well above the 160-character display limit. Google truncates these mid-sentence in search results, cutting off the key selling points before travellers can read them. The Rooms page description is a particularly severe 371 characters.

Why it matters Meta descriptions are the text snippet that appears below your title in Google search results. When truncated, they often end on an incomplete thought rather than a clear call to action. A well-crafted 140-155 character description that ends on a compelling line can increase click-through rates by 15-30%.
