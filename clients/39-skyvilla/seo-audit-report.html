Auto-Generated Blog Descriptions Over 160 Chars

Two blog posts use WordPress-generated excerpts as meta descriptions (180-262 characters). Google truncates anything over 160 characters, cutting off the message mid-sentence in search results.

Recommended fixWrite a concise, hand-crafted 130-155 character description for each blog post that gives travellers a clear reason to click.

Blog Post Missing H1 Heading

The "Festive Getaways at Sky Villa" post uses an H2 as its main heading instead of an H1. This is the primary signal Google uses to understand what a page is about - missing it weakens the page's ranking potential.

Recommended fixChange the top heading on that post from H2 to H1 in the WordPress block editor. A 2-minute fix.
