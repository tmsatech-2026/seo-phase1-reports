Missing H1 Headings

6 of 8 pages have no H1 tag at all. Only the homepage and privacy policy include one.

Why it matters H1 tags are the primary on-page signal Google uses to understand what a page is about. Without an H1, Google must guess — and often ranks the wrong keyword (or none at all). For luxury accommodation, every page needs a clear H1 that includes location and category keywords.

→ Add a keyword-rich H1 to each page (e.g. "Luxury Self-Catering Cabins in Oudtshoorn, Klein Karoo")

Critical
62%

Meta Descriptions Too Short or Missing

5 of 8 pages have descriptions under 120 characters — or none at all. Google's optimal range is 120–160 characters.

Why it matters Meta descriptions are the single line of text that appears in Google search results below your page title. A short or missing description means Google writes its own — often pulling random body text that doesn't sell the property. Compelling descriptions directly increase click-through rates by 15–30%.

→ Rewrite all descriptions to 130–155 characters with clear benefit-driven copy including location keywords

Broken URL Slug — Contact Page

The Contact page URL is /swap-the-busy-city-life/ — a creative tagline, not a keyword.

Why it matters URL slugs are a ranking signal. When someone searches "LANDRANI contact" or "book Oudtshoorn lodge", Google looks at the URL for relevance. A slug like /swap-the-busy-city-life/ signals nothing to Google about the page's purpose, wasting a ranking opportunity.

→ Redirect to /contact/ or /book-oudtshoorn-lodge/ with a 301 redirect

Why This Matters for LANDRANI®

Organic search and AI visibility are increasingly important channels for luxury accommodation — here's what's at stake.

🔍

Google Search — Your Highest-Intent Channel

People searching "luxury lodge Oudtshoorn" or "Klein Karoo accommodation with hot tub" are actively looking to book — not casually browsing. But if your pages lack H1s and have thin descriptions, Google won't rank them competitively. You're leaving high-intent traffic to competitors who have spent 30 minutes fixing their metadata.

🤖

ChatGPT & AI Search — The New Discovery Channel

When someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity "what are the best luxury lodges near Oudtshoorn?", the AI reads your website to decide whether to mention LANDRANI®. Clear H1s, well-structured descriptions, and strong on-page signals dramatically increase the chance your lodge is cited in AI-generated answers — a channel growing faster than traditional SEO.

📈

Google Ads Gets More Expensive Without Good SEO

If you're running Google Ads, Google's Quality Score partly depends on landing page relevance — which includes H1s and metadata. Poor on-page SEO means higher cost-per-click for the same ads. Good SEO and good ads compound: the organic traffic you earn reduces your reliance on paid clicks and improves your overall cost of acquisition.

⭐

You Have the Reviews — But Google Can't Find You

LANDRANI® is rated #1 in Oudtshoorn on TripAdvisor with a 97% Booking.com score. That's exceptional social proof — but it lives behind third-party platforms. Better on-site SEO ensures that same trust and authority transfers to your own website, so you capture more direct bookings (higher margin, no OTA commission) through Google search.

Recommendations — Prioritised Action Plan

These fixes are ordered by impact. Most can be completed in a single working session.

1

Add H1 Tags to All 6 Pages

Each page needs one clear H1 that includes the page's primary keyword + location where relevant. For example, the Dining page should have an H1 like "Farm-to-Table Dining at LANDRANI® Lodge, Oudtshoorn" rather than no heading at all. This is a WordPress/Divi text edit — no developer required.

⏱ Effort: Low (1–2 hours)  |  🎯 Impact: High
2

Rewrite 5 Meta Descriptions to 130–155 Characters

Target pages: /luxury-cabins/, /dining/, /included-activities/, /experience-landrani/, and /privacy-policy/. Each description should include a unique benefit, a location reference (Klein Karoo / Oudtshoorn), and a soft call to action. In Divi / Yoast SEO this is a one-field update per page.

⏱ Effort: Low (1 hour)  |  🎯 Impact: High (15–30% CTR improvement)
3

Fix the Contact Page Slug

Redirect /swap-the-busy-city-life/ to /contact/ using a 301 redirect in WordPress. Update the internal navigation link to point to the new URL. This takes less than 5 minutes and immediately improves keyword relevance for the Contact page.

⏱ Effort: Very Low (5 minutes)  |  🎯 Impact: Medium
4

Strengthen Page Titles — Dining & Activities Pages

The Dining page title is just "Dining - LANDRANI®" (18 characters). A better title: "Klein Karoo Farm Dining & Lamb BBQ Experiences | LANDRANI® Lodge". Richer titles rank for more long-tail searches and look far more compelling in Google results, especially when competing with OTAs like Booking.com.

⏱ Effort: Low (30 minutes)  |  🎯 Impact: Medium
5

Add Schema Markup (Structured Data)

Adding LodgingBusiness and Accommodation schema tells Google — and AI search engines — exactly what LANDRANI® is, where it is, what it offers, and how to book. This is one of the strongest signals for appearing in AI-generated travel recommendations (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews). This is a one-time technical implementation.

⏱ Effort: Medium (3–4 hours)  |  🎯 Impact: High (AI citation + rich results)
