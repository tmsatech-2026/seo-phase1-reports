H1 Headings Missing on Almost Every Page

7 of 11 pages have no H1 heading - including the accommodation overview, both room types, activities, packages, and about pages. The only pages with H1s are the homepage, FAQ, contact, and privacy policy. Google is ranking these pages without the clearest possible signal about their content.

Why it matters H1 tags are the primary on-page ranking signal. Without an H1 on the /our-rooms/ page, Google must guess whether it's about accommodation, a safari lodge, or a nature reserve. Adding a keyword-rich H1 like "Luxury Tented Suites at Kingfisher Creek | Guernsey Nature Reserve" would immediately strengthen that page's relevance for high-intent accommodation searches.

7/11

Meta Descriptions Too Long - Truncated in Search Results

7 pages have descriptions over 160 characters - including the rooms page (274 chars), activities (296 chars), about us (254 chars), and contact page (223 chars). Google cuts all of these off mid-sentence. The /our-specials/ page description is auto-generated body text ("Packages 2 Night Kruger Safari Includes:...") - not a proper description at all.

Why it matters When your description runs over 160 characters, Google shows the first ~155 chars and adds "..." - stopping the message at exactly the wrong moment. A well-crafted 150-160 char description that ends with a clear call to action (not mid-sentence) increases click-through rates by 15-30%. The packages page is a particular priority - replace the auto-generated text with a real description of the Kruger safari packages on offer.

Packages and Privacy Titles Too Short

The Packages page title "Packages | Kingfisher Creek Lodge" (33 chars) is critically short - missing Kruger, safari, and Limpopo keywords that would bring in high-intent traffic. The Privacy Policy (39 chars) is also below threshold. Both titles are below the 40-character minimum and score the lowest possible in Google's assessment of title quality.

Why it matters The /our-specials/ page is likely a key conversion page for guests considering a packaged safari. A 33-character title leaves 40+ characters unused. "Kruger Safari Packages | Kingfisher Creek Lodge Limpopo" (54 chars) would target searches like "Kruger safari package 2 nights" and "self-drive Kruger packages" - high-intent searches from guests who are actively comparing options.
