Every single page on karoo1.com is missing its meta description - a field Google uses to write the snippet shown in search results. 4 Page Titles Too Short (<40 Characters)

Medium

12 Titles in Suboptimal Length Range (40-49 Chars)

12 pages (67%)

The bulk of the site uses a boilerplate title pattern like "Page Name - Karoo 1 Hotel Accommodation" which produces titles in the 40-49 character warning zone. These titles lack keyword specificity - for example, "Rooms - Karoo 1 Hotel Accommodation" misses "Klein Karoo", "De Doorns", "farm hotel", and other terms guests actually search for.

Google Ads Quality Score is partly determined by your landing page experience - which includes SEO fundamentals like meta descriptions and title tags. Low Quality Scores mean higher cost-per-click and lower ad positions. Fixing your on-page SEO doesn't just improve organic rankings - it can directly reduce what you pay for every ad click to karoo1.com.
