All Titles Use Template Pattern Outside Optimal Range

Every page title follows the pattern "Property Name - West Coast Villa Rentals" which results in titles that are either too short (under 40 chars) or in the warning zone (40-49 chars). None are in the optimal 50-70 char range. Titles are the strongest ranking signal Google has - this pattern wastes the opportunity entirely.

Why it matters A title like "Moon House - Luxury Beachfront Villa, Britannia Bay | West Coast" (64 chars) tells Google and travellers exactly what the property is, where it is, and what category it belongs to. The current "Moon House - West Coast Villa Rentals" (37 chars) gives Google almost no ranking signal for "Britannia Bay villa" or "luxury beachfront accommodation" searches.

Our Villas Page Shows WordPress Theme Default as Title

The primary villa listing page at /our-villas/ - the most important landing page for anyone browsing the collection - has the title "Listing with Paging - West Coast Villa Rentals". This is a WordPress/WP Lister theme default. If a Google Ad sends traffic here, this is the title Google evaluates for Quality Score.

Why it matters "Listing with Paging" communicates nothing about the 28 luxury villas on the page. The correct title should be something like "Luxury Villa Rentals - West Coast South Africa | Browse All Properties" (67 chars). This single fix - changing one page title - would improve both organic ranking and Quality Score for the collection's most important page.
